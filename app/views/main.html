Launch and receive challenges

Cyf makes challenging people over many games a breeze.

1 - Make some Friends

Find people on Cyf who play the same games as you, or invite your facebook/twitter's friends to join you.

Cyf is fun when you have many friends, so we encourage you sending friend requests!

Read more

2 - Launch or Receive Challenges

For any challenge thrown at you, you'll receive a mail and a notification on Cyf. You can either accept or deny any invitation. Careful: can you handle many challenges at a time?

Launching a challenge is really easy: select a challenge, pick a friend and choose when do it have to start...That's it!

3 - Validate

When you complete a challenge, take a screenshot as a proof and just come back to send the validation request, done!

Once a challenger has completed a challenge, he will send a validation request and you will have to review if he/she did successfuly passed your challenge.
